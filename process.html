How I work

Process

Whether you need a single email template or a full lifecycle campaign system — here’s my structured approach to ensure results.

01

Discovery

A focused 30-minute call to understand your business goals, platform setup, audience, and timeline. I ask the right questions to uncover requirements and potential challenges — no commitment required.

02

Proposal

A clear, written scope of work outlining deliverables, timeline, and investment. All expectations are agreed upfront to avoid surprises and ensure alignment.

03

Delivery

Iterative project execution with regular check-ins and early previews. You can give feedback at every stage. I work asynchronously and communicate clearly to keep your team in the loop.

04

Handoff

Clean, fully documented deliverables with QA included for all email campaigns. Training or documentation is provided where needed to ensure your team can continue independently.
