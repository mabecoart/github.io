How I work

Process

Whether you need a single email template or a full campaign system — here's how we get there together.

01

Discovery

A short call (30 min) to understand your goals, platform setup, audience, and timeline. I ask the right questions so nothing gets missed. No commitment required.

02

Proposal

A clear, written scope of work with deliverables, timeline, and project investment. Everything is agreed before any work begins — no surprises.

03

Delivery

Iterative delivery with regular check-ins. You see progress early and can give feedback at each stage. I work asynchronously and communicate clearly throughout.

04

Handoff

Clean, documented deliverables. QA included on all email work. Training or documentation provided where needed.
