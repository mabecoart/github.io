01 · Overview

Project Overview

I led the development and optimization of over 200 responsive email campaigns across six brands under Intact Insurance. My work focused on creating efficient, consistent, and high-performing digital communications by standardizing HTML, CSS, and AMPscript code, implementing automation, and ensuring pixel-perfect rendering during the Salesforce Marketing Cloud migration. I collaborated closely with IT teams, marketing advisors, internal stakeholders, and external agencies to align email strategies and deliver measurable impact.

02 · Process

Project Process

01

Defined campaign scope, risks, and estimated timelines in collaboration with Product Owners using Microsoft Planner and Agile principles.

02

Design & Conception

Created master templates, modular email systems, and asset libraries to support scalable and standardized production in Salesforce Marketing Cloud.

03

Development & Testing

Developed responsive campaigns using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and AMPscript. Ensured high-quality rendering and deliverability through Litmus and multi-device QA validation.

04

05

Deployment & Review

Managed end-to-end deployment and monitored campaign performance, providing detailed reports and optimization recommendations to Product Owners.
