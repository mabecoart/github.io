01 · Overview

Project Overview

I designed and built responsive email campaigns and landing pages in Salesforce Marketing Cloud. My work supported the company’s migration to a new marketing automation platform, ensuring consistent design, accessibility, and performance across multiple audiences and devices across four product lines — Health Insurance, Travel Insurance, Life Insurance, and Group Insurance.

02 · Process

Project Process

01

Kickoff & Planning

Defined goals and requirements with project managers using Asana, Miro boards, and Agile sprint cycles.

02

Design & Conception

Designed flexible layouts for emails and landing pages in Figma, ensuring alignment with brand guidelines.

03

Development & Testing

Developed responsive emails and landing pages using HTML, CSS, JS, and AMPscript. Performed rendering and deliverability testing across major clients using Email on Acid and multiple web browsers for landing pages.

04

Launch & Optimization

Delivered production-ready assets, documented process improvements, and shared optimization insights with the internal team.

05

Deployment & Review

Developed and deployed automated multichannel journeys and manual email campaigns. Monitored campaign performance and provided detailed reports and insights to Product Owners.
