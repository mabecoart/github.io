01 · Overview

Project Overview

As part of Desjardins’ marketing automation transition, I led the development and integration of email campaign production methodologies in Oracle Eloqua, introducing new standards for collaboration, design, and delivery. The goal was to modernize workflows and increase production quality.

02 · Process

Project Process

01

Kickoff & Planning

Estimated timelines for building and sending email campaigns in collaboration with Product Owners, implementing Agile sprint cycles.

02

Design & Conception

Created modular templates and asset libraries in Figma to streamline campaign design.

03

Development & Testing

Developed campaigns using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring pixel-perfect rendering through Litmus testing and cross-browser validation for landing pages.

04

Launch & Optimization

Delivered production-ready assets, documented process improvements, and shared optimization insights with the internal team.

05

Deployment & Review

Developed and deployed automated multichannel journeys. Monitored campaign performance and provided detailed reports and insights to Product Owners.
